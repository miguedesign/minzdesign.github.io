Summary

Free Online Optical Shop Software (FOOSS) is the name of the app/tool developed by members of the Academy of physics FCFM , which objective to promote the teaching and research of optical tests that ronchigramas simulates and bi-ronchigramas and evaluated bi-ronchigramas of spherical surfaces.

After making a first prototype we tested with some users (students) and the feedback was key to develop the MVP of the tool that is currently online.
